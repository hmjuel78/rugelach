BAKED MAGIC

We create a unique rugelach by utilizing modern baking techniques and decades old tradition. Triangles of dough are coated with chocolate or cinnamon and rolled into tight swirls. Then, they are brushed with a sugary glaze that seeps into the dough, creating a dense and 'wet' pastry. The rugelach are then baked to perfection, removed from the oven just as they start to turn a golden brown.
